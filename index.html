Welcome to Salmon Cookies

Salmon cookies: Taste even better when enjoyed with a glass of milk!

Salmon cookies were originally created by hungry college students who had no prior culinary experience. One day a group of students got together and baked cookies with smoked salmon. It started as a Friday night fun activity, but the news spread out the entire campus that the combination of smoked salmon and cookies are incredible. Soon it was a cover story on Times magazine and it started like that.

Currently there are five locations where you can visit and purchase your favoriate salmon cookies. The store opens from 6am to 8pm. Single salmon cookie costs $15.00 but you can have 90% discount when purchase 5 or more at once.
